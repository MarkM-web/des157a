Hello and welcome to the game of pig.

Here are the rules of the game: Playes take turns to roll the dice. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score.

After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a “one” was not rolled) or pass to the other player.

If a player rolls a “one”, their turn is over, and it is the other player's turn.

If a player rolls two “ones” (snake eyes), that player's current score gets zeroed out.

The first player to get 30 points or higher wins.

Press "Start" to the bottom left to start the game
